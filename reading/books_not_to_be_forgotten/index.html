Books Not to be Forgotten: 2010—2020

(inspired by Sandy at Daedalus Bookshop)

This ongoing list is a collection of books, fiction and non-fiction, I have read over the past decade. Inspired by Sandy McAdams, owner of Daedalus Books in Charlottesville, VA, who keeps a hand-written list of books that are 'Not necessarily recommendations, but are not to be forgotten.' Featured up top in name only is The New Yorker magazine, which I have continually subscribed to since April 2018.

"(x)"s mark re-reads.

Reviews are linked.
