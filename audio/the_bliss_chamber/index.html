The Bliss Chamber is a narrative/sonic experiment, inviting you to imagine an environment in which your problems have gone away, simply in virtue of your willful ignorance. To hear and to experience this environment is my goal in this project, as well as to suggest why this approach is no longer sustainable. Music by Jean Glas.
