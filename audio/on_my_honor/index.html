This is On My Honor, a mini-series consisting of interviews and research done in 2017 that takes a hard look into the history of academic and personal integrity at UVA. The Honor Code is a pledge by which which all students swear on our honor, not to lie, cheat, or steal while at the University. This code has been part of the University for nearly all of its 200-year history. These interviews will reveal that UVA’s perception of honor may be an illusion – that maybe we’re not living in the honorary academic sphere we think we are.
